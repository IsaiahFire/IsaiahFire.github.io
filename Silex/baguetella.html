Baguetella
In this short film, we follow the story of Baguetella, a young baguette stuck living with her cruel stepmother. All Baguetella wants is to go to the ball - and with some magical intervention from the Sourdough Sorceress, she just might make it.

Produced at the end of my 10th grade year, Baguetella has been my first serious stop-motion animated film. I decided to take a well-known plot (Cinderella) and reimagine it. During this intensive process, after weeks of storyboarding and preparation - including baking miniature baguettes and constructing miniature felt hats, I stop-motion animated hundreds of frames to compose the base for my film, then added in music, dialogue, backgrounds, and special effects to bring the project to life. The result? A three-minute masterpiece that condenses the story of Cinderella, makes it really weird, and adds some Googly eyes.
